About Us

Welcome to Anjana Little Friends Preschool, a place where little minds embark on a delightful journey of learning, growth, and friendship. We believe that every child is a unique individual with limitless potential, and our preschool is dedicated to nurturing their budding talents in a warm and caring environment.



Vision

To nurture a capable child enriched with a moral mindset, creative heart and powerful arms to initiate a contributing citizen to the world.

Mission

Associating the discipline, talents, and capabilities of the children to align them with the contemporary digital era to fabricate them with the knowledge and skills necessary to become citizens who are creative, wise, morally upright, and endowed with positive attitudes

Briatris Wijesooriya

D. M. S. Ramyamali Wijesooriya is a distinguished individual who stands as the founder and creator of Anjana Little Friends. With an illustrious career spanning over 30 years, she has served as a dedicated dancing teacher within the government education sector.

Under Ramyamali Wijesooriya's guidance, children at Anjana Little Friends Preschool not only gain a strong foundation in intellectual development but also have the opportunity to explore their artistic potential through extra activities.

Shaping little minds for a better future

At Anjana Little Friends Preschool, we understand the importance of early childhood education in shaping a child's future. Our team of dedicated and experienced educators are passionate about creating a foundation for lifelong learning, ensuring that each child receives the support and guidance they need to thrive.

Our curriculum is thoughtfully designed to foster cognitive, social, emotional, and physical development. Through a variety of engaging activities, hands-on experiences, and interactive learning, we ignite curiosity, spark imagination, and encourage critical thinking skills in our students.

Services

25 Years of Reputation

Celebrating 25 years of excellence, Anjana Little Friends Preschool has established a reputation for nurturing little minds, fostering creativity, and providing a strong foundation for lifelong learning.

Qualified Teachers

Our preschool is proud to have a team of highly qualified teachers who are dedicated to creating a nurturing and engaging learning environment for your child's holistic development.

Best Education

At Anjana Little Friends Preschool, we are committed to providing the best education for your child, combining academic excellence, innovative teaching methods, and a nurturing environment to foster their love for learning.

Extra Curricular Activities

Beyond academics, we offer a range of enriching extracurricular activities at Anjana Little Friends Preschool. From art and music to sports and dance, we cultivate well-rounded development and joyful exploration.

Fun Times

At Anjana Little Friends Preschool, we believe in creating fun times for our little learners. Through engaging activities, trips, and laughter, we make every day a joyful adventure of learning and growing together.

Discipline and Behavior

Discipline and behavior are key values at our Preschool. We foster a supportive and structured environment, instilling positive values, respectful conduct, and self-discipline to help children thrive academically and socially.
